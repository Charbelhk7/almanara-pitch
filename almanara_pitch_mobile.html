Initial Diagnosis

What I suspect are four gaps. All structural. All fixable.

These are working hypotheses for discussion, not assumptions presented as facts.

01 — Visibility
Low pipeline visibility
Pipeline likely lives across Excel, inboxes, and WhatsApp. Real-time visibility is low. Forecast accuracy is likely under 50%.
High impact
02 — Process
Execution depends on reps
Core steps likely depend on memory, not systems. Follow-up is inconsistent. Momentum loss could easily be costing 15–25% of active opportunities.
Revenue risk
03 — Outreach
Prospecting is uneven
Outreach appears manual and inconsistent. Response times vary. New pipeline creation depends on who has time, not on a repeatable commercial engine.
Growth blocker
04 — Learning Loop
Loss reasons are not usable
Wins and losses are likely not captured in a structured way. That means no reliable feedback loop to improve targeting, follow-up, or conversion over time.

The Vision

Two Mondays.

Monday morning — today

  • “How did last week go?” — I am asking a rep because I cannot see it directly.
  • “I think the pipeline is healthy.” — I have no consistent way to verify that.
  • A quote went out last week. I do not know whether follow-up happened.
  • We lost a deal last month. I still do not know why it was lost.
  • There are probably leads sitting untouched. I cannot surface them in minutes.

Monday morning — 12 weeks from now

  • I open my phone. Pipeline value. Deals closing. Deals at risk.
  • Every rep’s activity is visible. Follow-up is automated.
  • Lost deals are logged with reasons. Priorities for the week are clear.
  • New prospects are entering the pipeline through a repeatable process.
  • The commercial side is no longer dependent on memory and guesswork.
